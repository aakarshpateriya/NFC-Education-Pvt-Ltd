Why NFC Exists ?

Empowering Sports:

NFC aims to promote sports activities among youth, fostering physical health and well-being.
By providing resources, facilities, and training programs, NFC encourages participation in various sports, nurturing talent and promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Enhancing Education:

NFC is committed to improving educational opportunities for children and young adults.
Through initiatives such as scholarships, tutoring programs, and educational workshops, NFC supports academic excellence and lifelong learning.

How NFC Works ?

Needs Assessment and Planning:

NFC begins by conducting thorough needs assessments to identify the specific challenges and opportunities within the community.
Based on these assessments, NFC collaborates with stakeholders to develop comprehensive strategic plans tailored to address the identified needs effectively.
