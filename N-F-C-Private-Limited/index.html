NFC's History

The club, established in the year 2021 by the visionary founders of NFC, Ayush and Aakarsh, came into existence with a clear and inspiring mission: to provide the youth of the country with a unique platform that combines sports and education. This innovative approach is a testament to the club's commitment to the holistic development of young individuals, fostering their physical, mental, and social growth.
